We Need You

HAVE WRITTEN IN 31/07/2023


Cydra InfSkl, also known as Cydra Information Forum School, is a platform created to help young people access the world and have a comprehensive view of life. It is also a place for parents to better understand their children and share valuable experiences that are hard to come by. Although Cydra is already completed, due to a shortage of staff, I would like to address this issue today.

We are looking for young individuals with potential, passion, and a willingness to develop their skills to contribute to the common future. If you are concerned about your education or lack confidence, rest assured that Cydra has dedicated and friendly former members to guide you, completely free of charge. Age is not important, as long as you are passionate, Cydra will support you wholeheartedly. As for equipment, you only need a phone, and having a laptop or computer is even better.

If you would like to register, please visit the following link: https://cydra-infskl.io.vn/list-admin/tuyen-member/index.html

Regarding privileges:
- You will have the opportunity to develop yourself and learn more about management, documentation, administration, security, and construction.
- You will be proud of your dedication, as will your family and everyone else.
- You will leave behind beautiful memories of your time as a student.
